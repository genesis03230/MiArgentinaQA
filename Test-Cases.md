## TC-01: Pick Up and Place Province on the Map

**Test Type:** Manual  
**Test Level:** Gameplay / Functional  
**Priority:** High  

### Preconditions
- Game is launched successfully
- Player is inside the main puzzle room
- At least one province collectible is available in the scene

### Steps
1. Aim at a province using the raycast interaction
2. Pick up the province
3. Move the province towards the Argentina map
4. Place the province onto the correct position on the map

### Expected Result
- The province snaps correctly into the map
- The province is no longer interactable
- The game correctly updates progression toward completion
